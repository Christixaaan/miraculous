Skript zu einer Szene aus Staffel 4

*At the park, Marinette watches Tikki eating cookies of someone's picnic. We can recognize the bakery boxes. When the last person of the picnic leaves, Marinette approaches Tikki who's still eating*

Marinette: Tikki, you're a kwami! No one can see you! You can't stay there, come here!

*Tikki stops eating and her face is normal again*

Tikki: Sorry Marinette, I couldn't control myself.

Marinette: You need to control yourself, my family has been akumatized and I need you!

Tikki: Can't you see? There's only one piece left. If I eat it, there will be nothing left!

*Tikki's expression changes and her voice turns weird*

Tikki: I know how to get more, I'm the kwami of creation!

*Marinette realizes and tries to stop her*

Marinette: Oh no! A kwami using their power with no holder always causes a catastrophe!

Tikki: Lucky Charm!

*Plagg looks at the gaint cookie*

Plagg: It looks like Tikki got a bit crazy for chocolate again.

Adrien: A bit?

Plagg: The last time it happened was 1000 years ago, when humans started making chocolate.

Adrien: And what happened?

Plagg: It rained chocolate cream for a week.

*Adrien is worried*

Plagg: No need to worry! There's a reason why the kwami of destruction exists. Let's go Adrien! Transform!

Adrien: Okay then, Plagg claws out!
